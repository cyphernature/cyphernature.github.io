Knowledge and Nature

Cypher Nature's mission is to try and empower people through the education and use of Art, Culture, and Technology following akin to a STEAM model.

Our Goal is to create quality content on a limited budget. Our intent is to show that it can be done, and how it can be done. Our purpose is to use this knowledge shared freely to encourage other artists or potential artists to create their content and massage their message where it might previously gone unsaid given a lack of resources which are not privileged to many potential artists and story writers.

Additional goals - after creating this website I plan to search for work in a technical job which helps society -- specifically those who might otherwise be Othered by society. Videography, 3D Modeling and Animation, flyers -- logos and so on. For those who need it, with intention to make enough to make rent, feed my belly, fuel my car and pay my bills.
